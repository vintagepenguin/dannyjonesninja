Who I am

I'm a left-handed former collegiate runner who works as an engineer at MX. I do really random adventures. I drink way more chocolate milk than any human should. I only do posivibes--no neggyvibes. And if you ever catch me dancing at a friend's wedding you'll see I'm kinda like Shakira: my hips don't lie.


I'm really goofy, but I'm always serious when it comes to taking care of a few things: my faith, my family, and my friends.

Mormon

I’m a Mormon. But what does that mean? First and foremost, it means I believe in following Jesus Christ. But what does that even mean? I think my brother, Ryan Jones, said it best when he said, "The purpose of the Gospel of Jesus Christ is to improve yourself and love others, not to love yourself and improve others.”


If you're curious about my beliefs, connect with me I'd be happy to answer any questions y'all have.

Runner

It started in elementary school. My entire grade raced and I won. I kept winning in junior high, and then into high school. Then, I had the chance to run in college.

I got into running because I won. But I run today because it’s taught me to push myself, to think for myself and to stretch myself.

Writer

I was not happy. My teacher had just given us an assignment: write a weekly blog. I hated writing. There were two rules for the assignment, write once a week and write about whatever you want.

Turns out, I don’t hate writing—I just hate not choosing what I write. Since that assignment, I’ve loved writing, and do it as much as I can.

Developer

I studied and graduated in business from the Marriott School at BYU. But my junior year I realized I was passionate about something else: programming. I did it that summer, and right after I graduated I moved to chicago to do DevBootcamp. After that, I moved back to Utah where I now work.

My languages: ruby + rails framework, sql, html, css, and some javascript.
